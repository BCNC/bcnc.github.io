Welcome To BCNC

The Business Career and Networking Club was created to bridge the gap between businesses and UC Davis students. We strive to link qualified individuals with innovative companies by working year-round to bring value to our students and their future employers. By hosting two major events per quarter, we provide students with valuable resources to build their professional skills and network with companies, organizations, and young professionals.
