FAQ for Students

How do I become a general member of the Business Career & Networking Club?

Unlike most student organizations, the Business Career & Networking Club does not offer general membership and consists solely of an Executive Board. However, all of our events and services are completely free and available to all students at UC Davis!

When can I apply to become a member of the Executive Board?

The Business Career & Networking Club is unique in that we operate like a small startup company. Our recruitment needs are determined by the number of open board positions and our demand for specific job functions. Although most of our recruitment takes place in the Fall and Spring quarters, BCNC does not follow a fixed onboarding schedule.

FAQ for Undergraduate Business Organizations

We are a new student business group at UC Davis and would like to become a BCNC Partner Organization! How do we get started?

Thank you for your interest in becoming a BCNC Partner! We would love to learn more about your organization before discussing potential partnership. To get started, please reach out to our email at contactbcnc@gmail.com with “Partner Organization Request” as your subject line.

What are the benefits of becoming a BCNC Partner Organization?

Great question! As a BCNC Partner Organization, you will be able to advertise your upcoming events on our Weekly Newsletter, participate in our Brand Ambassadors Program and student networking events, and much more!

FAQ for Professionals

How do we get involved with the Business Career & Networking Club?

Our team provides marketing services to our professional organizations through career fairs, information sessions, and social media posts. For specific requests or inquiries, please reach out to our email at contactbcnc@gmail.com.
