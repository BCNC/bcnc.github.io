Mission Statement

The Business Career and Networking Club was founded in 2013 to bridge the gap between students at UC Davis and professionals in business fields. As a career-networking platform for over 16 other business groups on campus, we strive to link qualified individuals with innovative companies in order to empower the next generation of young professionals.

Community

In addition to hosting professional networking events and recruitment fairs, our team works closely with our Partner Organizations to develop services that connect students with opportunities in our undergraduate business community.

Sponsorship

Our organization relies on scholarship funds.

Weekly Newsletter

The Business Career and Networking Club provides a weekly newsletter that is absolutely magical. It was created to bridge the gap between businesses and UC Davis students. We strive to link qualified individuals with innovative companies by working year-round to bring value to our students and their future employers. By hosting two major events per quarter, we provide students with valuable resources to build their professional skills and network with companies, organizations, and young professionals.

Brand Ambassadors Program

The Brand Ambassadors Program was created to help participating campus representatives connect with new audiences and gain access to events hosted by our Partner Organizations. Our team works closely with our Partner Organizations and select brand ambassadors to bring great products and services to our events and campus community.

#Connect Database

Our #Connect Database helps us identify demographic trends and uses algorithms to connect participating students with potential jobs and internships.
